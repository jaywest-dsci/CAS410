Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. This prevented her from being able to go to school. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. With the help of the new well, Amina can now go to school. Not only did it affect her life but now her family is also healthier and happier
